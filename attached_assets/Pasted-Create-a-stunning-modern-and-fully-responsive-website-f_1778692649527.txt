Create a stunning, modern, and fully responsive website for **BlueHazy FM**, a professional online radio station. The website must have a premium media-platform appearance with a realistic radio broadcasting atmosphere. Use a beautiful combination of **blue and white colors** with subtle dark accents to create a clean, energetic, and futuristic design. The overall website should feel immersive, modern, community-focused, and visually engaging for both mobile and desktop users.

The website should include realistic AI-generated images of:

* Radio presenters and DJs
* Podcast/interview setups
* Music studio environments
* Live broadcasting scenes
* Community events and nightlife
* Youth culture and entertainment
* Professional radio equipment and microphones

Use high-quality visuals, modern layouts, smooth animations, glowing UI effects, gradients, glassmorphism sections, hover effects, and audio-inspired design elements such as equalizer animations, sound waves, and dynamic lighting.
